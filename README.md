# Hello, I'm Reem

# Objective

I’m an aspiring SOC Analyst with a strong foundation in computer programming and core cybersecurity knowledge backed by my Network+ and Security+ certifications. Over the past three years, I’ve built a professional network within the cybersecurity community at Fidelity Investments, where mentorship and continuous feedback helped shape my technical growth and career direction.

Through these connections, I gained exposure to real-world security operations, collaborated on small-scale penetration testing projects, shadowed experienced analysts, and learned how to translate technical findings into actionable recommendations. These experiences strengthened my passion for cybersecurity and helped me develop practical skills in threat detection, network security, and incident response.

I’m committed to continuous learning, hands-on practice, and contributing to teams that value proactive defense and strong security culture. My goal is to bring my technical foundation, curiosity, and growing SOC experience into an environment where I can make a meaningful impact while continuing to advance my skills.

Specifically aiming to join a Security Operations Center (SOC) as a Tier 1 Analyst.
